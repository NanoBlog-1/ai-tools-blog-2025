About this site

This site explains a keyword strategy and workflow that works with AI—for e‑commerce product and category pages, and for content marketing (blogs, guides). The goal is to help teams prioritize what to optimize or write first, then use ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini with ready-made prompts and templates.

Who it’s for

E‑commerce teams with many product or category pages, content and growth teams publishing articles, and anyone using AI to create content who wants a clear keyword backbone so that content can rank. The workflow fits any market or language.

What we offer

Prompt packs for keyword clustering and SERP analysis, Notion and Google Sheet templates, and a repeatable process. One-time purchase gives lifetime access; a subscription adds new prompt packs each month. Payments are via card (Stripe), Apple Pay, or Solana.
