Premium: AI Keyword Tools

This guide walks you through a repeatable keyword process: cluster keywords by intent with AI, analyze SERPs for each cluster, then score competition and prioritize what to write. Use the templates and prompts below in your own tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Notion, Sheets).

Methodology
  1. Cluster -- Group keywords by intent (informational, commercial, transactional) and track them.
  2. SERP -- For each cluster, see what ranks and what content type wins.
  3. Score & prioritize -- Decide what to optimize or write first (difficulty, opportunity, priority).
  4. Map -- Tie clusters to your site (pillar pages, subpages, target URLs).
  5. Ship -- Use the prompt packs below in ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini to create and optimize.
